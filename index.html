Privacy Policy for WExcel

Last updated: September 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. All WhatsApp messages processed by the app remain on your device and are only converted into Excel format at your request.

2. In-App Purchases

WExcel offers optional one-time in-app purchases to unlock additional features. All purchases are processed securely through the Google Play Store, and we do not collect or store your payment or billing information. Google may collect certain information as part of the purchase process, in accordance with their Google Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and location information for ad targeting in accordance with their own privacy policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases are handled securely by the Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

4. GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the EEA, you have rights under GDPR, including access, correction, deletion, and objection to data processing. Since WExcel does not collect personal data, these rights generally do not apply. Any data processed by AdMob is subject to their policies.

5. CCPA & CPRA (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data. WExcel does not sell data. Any information collected via ads is handled by Google AdMob.

6. CalOPPA

This Privacy Policy is available at all times. Updates will be posted with a new effective date. We do not collect personal data directly from users.

7. Children’s Privacy

WExcel is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents can restrict ad personalization via Google settings.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
